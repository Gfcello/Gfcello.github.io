Welcome to my collection of robots!

(and other work)

Hi, I'm Gordon, and I make a lot of robots. Earlier this year I worked on a marine rescue robot that uses GPS and a waterproofed thermal camera to locate overboard victims in open water, navigate to them, provide flotation, and guide rescue efforts to them. I am also working on a snake robot that is over a meter long and gives me a great base to investigate more complicated collision detection and avoidance algorithms in addition to developing organic motions and climbing sequences.

While my specialty is firmware and controls, I'm an obsessive generalist who does everything from mechanical design to electrical. Check out all of my work!

I recently graduated from Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I've worked at places like Impossible Metals, Kindred (Ocado), and Skygauge Robotics.
